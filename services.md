# Personal Energy Coaching
A mechanical engineer from Johns Hopkins turned energy coach in Hawaii, Alexandra helps people unite their logical minds with their intuitive hearts to design the life they desire. She teaches people to work intentionally with their own energy, using thoughts, emotions, and the physical body—the most powerful tools for transformation.

<h2>Structure</h2>
<ul>
  <li>10 individual coaching sessions</li>
  <li>Sessions are guided by your current goals, rather than a fixed curriculum</li>
</ul>

<h2>Core Focus</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Energy boosting practices to reduce chronic overload and stress</li>
  <li>Coaching questions that bring awareness to how your internal state (body, emotions, attention) interacts with your external environment</li>
  <li>Practical energy tools and techniques that integrate easily in your daily life</li>
</ul>

<h2>You will be guided to hone…</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Ability to increase energy quickly and easily</li>
  <li>Awareness of what fuels vs. drains your energy</li>
</ul>
